Biography

I am a research fellow at the College of Computing and Data Science (School of Computer Science and Engineering till April 2024), Nanyang Technological University. Previously, I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University in 2023, advised by Prof. Cheng Long. I received my bachelor's degree from Shandong University in 2018.

My general research interest lies in graph data mining and management, particularly cohesive subgraph mining and graph algorithms.
